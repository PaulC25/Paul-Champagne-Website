Welcome!

Hi, my name is Paul Champagne, and this is my personal website.

I'm a fourth year student at Northeastern University, studying Finance and Data Science.

Since high school, I've always been interested in financial markets and was determined to pursue majoring in it as I entered college. As I started to gain more and more knowledge in the field, I realized how much data was involved in being able to analyze the price of stock, the state of the economy, or any other type of analysis I wanted to make. It didn't take long to realize that data science would be a great way to tackle the questions I had. Ever since, I’ve been keen in using programming to help answer those questions and research suitable investing strategies and theories. Through this website, I hope to show how I’ve been able to combine both subjects to create meaningful projects.

Overall, I'm passionate about exploring the intersection between both fields, and here you will find some of the projects I have worked on throughout the last couple years that showcase the skills I've obtained throughout my college and professional career.
